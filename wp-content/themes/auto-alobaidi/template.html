WELCOME TO LAITH AL OBAIDI

ALOBAIDI GROUP is a family business, established in 1968 in Iraq, by Mr. Sahib Alobaidi the father of Mr. Laith Alobaidi.

Mr. Laith Alobaidi is an Iraqi Entrepreneur Businessman, born in 1967, he is the Co-founder & Chairman of Laith Alobaidi Group. Started his automotive career in 1985. Known as a landmark in cars trading in Iraq, the GCC region & Jordan. After improving the Automotive business all over the Iraq branches, in 2002 he had moved & settled in UAE, as a business man eager for more success and expanding, by opening the first branch in GCC, in United Arab of Emirates in 2002 Sharjah, & Dubai branch in 2003, then the rest of UAE, in same time of success, he has expanding the group by lunching Jordan branch in 2003.

Mr. Laith Alobaidi has worked on strengthening the export sector, promoting investments to create jobs and Speeding up economic Development. His brothers have come to the board of directors by starting their career under his supervision, Mr. Mohammed Alobaidi, in 1990. Mr. Ahmed Alobaidi in 1990. Who is supervising Jordan & Iraq region, and Mr. Ali Alobaidi 1991. All together they became a wholesaler’s dealing in new and used passenger and company cars and supplier of remarketing services. Over the years Laith Alobaidi have continued to adapt itself to changing market conditions making it a company with a stable position and an excellent reputation. In order to maintain lasting future prospects, we think about our future position on a daily basis. We find transparency important in any case, and that’s why we’ll gladly share our vision, mission and our company culture with you!
